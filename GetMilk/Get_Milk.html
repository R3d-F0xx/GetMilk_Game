Get Milk: The HTML Choose Your Own Adventure

Welcome to Get Milk, a single page html choose your own adventure story where you've run out of milk to drink.

Prolouge

You're enjoying your favorite game on Newgrounds, when you start to get thirsty for a glass of milk. You walk to your fridge, and, to your mild dismay, find no milk in the fridge, but rather emptied in the recycle bin. What is your next move?
